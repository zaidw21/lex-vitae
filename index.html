Company Overview

Lex Vitae Solicitors and Consultants

Lex Vitae Legal Services is a complete legal solution Law Firm from Central India having office at Indore and associate offices at Bhopal, Nagpur, Mumbai and Delhi.

Established by the alumnus of National Law University with an aim to provide professional and ethical legal services to its client.

“Lex Vitae” is working towards development of successful legal strategies with the optimum utilization of skills & ethical behavior to provide best available legal support to the system, courts and clients.

“Lex Vitae” specializes in Litigation (Civil & Criminal), Alternate Dispute Resolution, Company Matters, Statutory Compliances, Labour and Corporate Legal Consultation.

“Lex Vitae” is offering comprehensive legal services through its self-owned office and with associate offices in Maharashtra, Delhi, Karnataka, Punjab, Haryana & Chhattisgarh as well as areas around these locations.
